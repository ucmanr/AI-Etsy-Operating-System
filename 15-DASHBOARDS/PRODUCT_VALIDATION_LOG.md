# Product Validation Log (Template)

## Purpose
To maintain a historical record of all product ideas, their validation scores, and their ultimate outcome. This trains the AI (and the founder) on what types of ideas actually succeed.

## Rule
Every rejected idea must be logged. This prevents the AI or founder from suggesting the same bad idea twice.
