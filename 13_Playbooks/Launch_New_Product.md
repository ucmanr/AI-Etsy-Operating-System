# Launch New Product Playbook

## Objective
Launch a validated Etsy product with the highest probability of success.

## Workflow

1. Validate demand
2. Define customer problem
3. Create MVP
4. Design assets
5. QA review
6. Optimize SEO
7. Publish
8. Measure results after 3, 7, and 30 days
9. Record lessons
10. Decide whether to improve, expand, or retire

## Success Criteria

- Product published
- Listing indexed
- Metrics recorded
