# Master Playbook: Digital Product Lifecycle

This is the standard operating procedure for every digital product in this company. No product skips a phase.

## Phase 1: Ideation
- **Input**: Market gaps, competitor 1-star reviews, trend data.
- **Process**: Brainstorm 10 raw concepts.
- **Output**: List of 10 raw ideas.
- **Quality Gate**: Ideas must align with the ADHD productivity niche.

## Phase 2: Research & Validation
- **Input**: 10 raw ideas.
- **Process**: Run ideas through the Product Validation Engine (Demand, Competition, Trend, Profit, Difficulty scores).
- **Output**: 1-2 validated concepts with a Confidence Score >= 85/100.
- **Quality Gate**: Score < 85 = Reject and return to Phase 1.

## Phase 3: Product Briefing
- **Input**: Validated concept.
- **Process**: CEO/Product Manager creates a detailed spec (dimensions, features, target use-case, bundle potential).
- **Output**: Approved Product Brief.

## Phase 4: Design & QA
- **Input**: Product Brief.
- **Process**: Designer creates assets. QA Reviewer checks against Cognitive Load and Accessibility rules.
- **Output**: Final, QA-approved digital asset (PDF, PNG, etc.).
- **Quality Gate**: QA fails = Return to Designer.

## Phase 5: Etsy Listing & SEO
- **Input**: Final asset.
- **Process**: SEO Agent generates title, 13 tags, and description. Listing Optimizer creates 10 mockup images.
- **Output**: Live, fully optimized Etsy listing.

## Phase 6: Launch & Marketing
- **Input**: Live listing.
- **Process**: Trigger Pinterest pin sequence, add to email welcome flow, run initial traffic test.
- **Output**: Initial traffic and first 7-day data.

## Phase 7: Analytics & Optimization
- **Input**: 14-day listing data (Views, Favorites, Conversion Rate).
- **Process**: Analyze metrics. If Conversion Rate < 1%, optimize images/tags. If > 3%, create a bundle or complementary product.
- **Output**: Iterate, Bundle, or Kill decision.
