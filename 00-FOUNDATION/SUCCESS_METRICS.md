# Success Metrics & KPIs

## 1. Revenue KPIs
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Target $3,000 by Day 90.
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Target $15+ (driven by bundling).
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Target < $2 (via organic Etsy/Pinterest).

## 2. Product KPIs
- **Validation Pass Rate**: > 20% of ideated products pass the 85/100 score.
- **Time-to-Launch**: < 7 days from validated concept to live listing.
- **QA Pass Rate**: > 90% of designs pass on the first review.

## 3. Marketing & SEO KPIs
- **Etsy Conversion Rate**: Target > 2.5%.
- **Listing Views to Favorites Ratio**: Target > 10%.
- **Pinterest Monthly Outbound Clicks**: Target > 500 by Day 60.

## 4. Operational KPIs
- **Automation Success Rate**: > 95% of automated workflows complete without manual intervention.
- **SOP Adherence**: 100% of new processes must have a documented SOP before execution.
