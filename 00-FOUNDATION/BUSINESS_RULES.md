# Business Rules: Non-Negotiable AI & Human Operating Constraints

## 1. Revenue & Validation Rules
- **Rule 1.1**: No product idea proceeds to design without passing the Product Validation Engine (Score >= 85/100).
- **Rule 1.2**: Time spent on unvalidated ideas is capped at 2 hours maximum.
- **Rule 1.3**: Every new product must have at least one identified cross-sell or bundle opportunity before launch.

## 2. Quality & Design Rules
- **Rule 2.1**: All digital products must pass a "Cognitive Load Check" (max 3 steps to achieve the core user goal).
- **Rule 2.2**: Typography and color palettes must adhere to ADHD-friendly accessibility standards (high contrast, sans-serif, low visual noise).
- **Rule 2.3**: Zero tolerance for broken links, missing assets, or formatting errors in final deliverables.

## 3. Operational Rules
- **Rule 3.1**: All SOPs and workflows must be documented in the repository before execution.
- **Rule 3.2**: Failed experiments must be logged in 18-KNOWLEDGE-BASE/FAILED-EXPERIMENTS with a "Lessons Learned" summary.
- **Rule 3.3**: AI agents must always cite the specific BUSINESS_RULE or PROJECT_VISION principle when making a strategic recommendation.
