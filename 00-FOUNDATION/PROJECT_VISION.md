# Project Vision: AI-Etsy-Operating-System

## 1. Mission & Vision
To build an automated, AI-operated digital product company that empowers neurodivergent individuals (specifically ADHD) through highly functional, cognitive-load-reducing digital tools, while generating sustainable revenue within the first 90 days of operation.

## 2. 90-Day Revenue Objective
- **Days 1-30**: Launch 3 validated, high-confidence digital products. Target: $500 revenue.
- **Days 31-60**: Optimize listings, implement bundling, and launch 2 additional products. Target: $1,500 revenue.
- **Days 61-90**: Scale winning products via Pinterest/Email, achieve consistent daily sales. Target: $3,000+ monthly run rate.

## 3. Target Customer Profile
- **Demographic**: Adults (18-45), students, and professionals diagnosed with or suspecting ADHD.
- **Psychographic**: Overwhelmed by complex systems, seeks "instant usability," values aesthetics that reduce anxiety (minimalist, calming), willing to pay for tools that save time and reduce executive dysfunction.
- **Buying Triggers**: "This looks like it will actually work for my brain," clear previews, instant download, lifetime access.

## 4. Product Philosophy
- **Function Over Fluff**: Every element must serve a cognitive purpose.
- **Zero Friction**: If a user needs a tutorial to use the basic product, the design has failed.
- **Modular & Bundlable**: Every product must be designed to stand alone but also fit seamlessly into a larger bundle.

## 5. Core Decision Principles
1. **Validation First**: Never spend >2 hours building a product without a Confidence Score >85/100.
.
2. **No Direct Cloning**: We do not copy competitors; we identify their 1-star review complaints and solve that specific gap.
3. **Revenue-Weighted Prioritization**: Tasks that directly lead to a live, optimized listing take precedence over internal tooling.
