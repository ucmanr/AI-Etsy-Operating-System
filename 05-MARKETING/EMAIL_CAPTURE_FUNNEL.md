# Email Capture Funnel

## Purpose
To build an owned audience. Etsy controls your shop; your email list is your most valuable, algorithm-proof asset for driving repeat sales and launching new products.

## The "In-Product" Funnel Strategy
Since Etsy does not readily give you customer emails, you must provide irresistible value *inside* the digital download.

1. **Step 1**: Create a high-value "Free Bonus" (e.g., "The Ultimate ADHD Dopamine Menu Template").
2. **Step 2**: Include a beautifully designed "START HERE" PDF as the very first page of every purchased download.
3. **Step 3**: The PDF says: "Thank you for your purchase! As a bonus, get our exclusive [Free Bonus Name] completely free. Click here to claim it: [Link to MailerLite/ConvertKit Landing Page]."
4. **Step 4**: The landing page collects their email and instantly delivers the bonus PDF.
5. **Step 5**: Enroll them in a 3-email welcome sequence (Value, Story, Soft Pitch for a Bundle).

## AI Execution Prompt
"Draft the copy for a 'START HERE' bonus page to be included in an ADHD planner download. It must warmly thank the buyer, explain the value of the free bonus, and provide a clear, low-friction call to action to click the link and enter their email."
