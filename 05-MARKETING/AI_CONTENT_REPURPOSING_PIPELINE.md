# AI Content Repurposing Pipeline

## Purpose
To maximize the ROI of every product launch by turning one core asset into 10+ pieces of marketing content across multiple platforms.

## The "1-to-10" Workflow
**Input**: 1 Approved Etsy Listing Description + Product Mockups.

**Outputs**:
1. **1 Blog Post / Long-form Thread**: "5 Reasons Traditional Planners Fail ADHD Brains" - links to Etsy.
2. **3 Pinterest Pins**: Extract 3 key quotes/benefits from the description and overlay on mockups.
3. **2 TikTok/Reel Scripts**: Turn the "Problem/Solution" section of the description into a 15-second video script.
4. **4 Twitter/Threads Posts**: Break down the 4 main features into a short, punchy thread.
5. **1 Email Newsletter**: Announce the launch to the existing list using the "Empathy & Solution" hook.

## AI Execution Prompt
"Act as the Content Repurposing Engine. Take this Etsy listing description: '[PASTE DESCRIPTION]'. Generate: 1) One TikTok script (15 secs), 2) Three Pinterest pin text overlays, 3) A 4-part Twitter thread. Ensure all outputs are empathetic, ADHD-friendly, and drive traffic to the shop."
