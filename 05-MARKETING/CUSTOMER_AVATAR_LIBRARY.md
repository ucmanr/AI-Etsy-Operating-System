# Customer Avatar Library

## Purpose
To ensure every marketing decision, image, and sentence is tailored to the specific psychological profile of our buyer.

## Avatar 1: "The Overwhelmed Student"
- **Demographic**: 18-24, college/university.
- **Core Pain Point**: Missing deadlines, feeling paralyzed by large assignments, guilty about procrastination.
- **Emotional Trigger**: Fear of failure, feeling "stupid" or "lazy."
- **Buying Objection**: "I'll just abandon this planner like the last three."
- **Our Marketing Answer**: "Designed for *how your brain actually works*. Flexible, forgiving, and focused on progress, not perfection."

## Avatar 2: "The Neurodivergent Professional"
- **Demographic**: 25-45, working full-time, possibly diagnosed late in life.
- **Core Pain Point**: Dropping the ball on household tasks while managing work, mental fatigue by 6 PM.
- **Emotional Trigger**: Burnout, feeling like they are "faking it."
- **Buying Objection**: "I don't have time to learn a complex new system."
- **Our Marketing Answer**: "Set up in 30 seconds. Zero learning curve. Reclaim your evenings."
