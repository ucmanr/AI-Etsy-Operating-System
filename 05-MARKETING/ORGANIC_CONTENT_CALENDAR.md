# Organic Content Calendar

## Purpose
To maintain a consistent, low-friction social media presence that builds brand awareness and drives traffic without causing founder burnout.

## The "3-Day/Week" Sustainable Schedule
- **Monday (Educational/Value)**: Share a quick ADHD productivity tip, hack, or relatable struggle. Softly mention how the product helps. (Format: TikTok/Reels or Twitter Thread).
- **Wednesday (Aesthetic/Showcase)**: A beautiful, calming video or photo of the product in use. Focus on the *feeling* of being organized. (Format: Instagram Reel, TikTok, or Pinterest Idea Pin).
- **Friday (Social Proof/Community)**: Share a customer review, a "behind the scenes" of designing, or a relatable ADHD meme. (Format: Instagram Story, Twitter, or Pinterest Static Pin).

## The "Batching" Rule
Spend 2 hours on Sunday creating all 3 pieces of content for the week. Schedule them in advance. Do not create content daily.

## AI Execution Prompt
"Generate a 1-week organic content calendar (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) for '[PRODUCT NAME]'. For each day, provide: 1) Content format, 2) The core hook/message, 3) A short script or caption, 4) Relevant hashtags."
