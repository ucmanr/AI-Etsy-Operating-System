# Marketing Agent Prompts

## Purpose
Ready-to-use, highly engineered prompts for Manus AI to execute marketing tasks rapidly and consistently.

## Prompt 1: Full Listing Generation
"Act as the Etsy Marketing Agent. Using the Product Brief: '[PASTE BRIEF]', generate a complete Etsy listing package. Include: 1) One 140-char SEO title, 2) 13 long-tail tags, 3) A description using the Listing Copy Framework (Hook, Empathy, Bulleted Benefits, Friction Remover), 4) A 10-image mockup sequence description. Ensure tone is empowering and ADHD-friendly."

## Prompt 2: Competitor Teardown
"Analyze this competitor listing: [URL]. Extract their title structure, estimate their top 5 tags, critique their image sequence, and summarize their 1-3 star review complaints. Conclude with 3 specific ways our listing can outperform them."

## Prompt 3: Bundle Ideation
"Review these 3 existing products: [LIST PRODUCTS]. Propose a 'Starter Bundle' and an 'Ultimate System' bundle. For each, define the included items, the suggested bundle price (using our Pricing Strategy), and the core 'transformation' the bundle provides."

## Prompt 4: Review Response
"Draft a warm, professional, and empathetic response to this customer review: '[PASTE REVIEW]'. If negative, offer a direct solution. If positive, express genuine gratitude and reinforce the brand's mission."
