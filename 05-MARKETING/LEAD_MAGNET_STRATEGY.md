# Lead Magnet Strategy

## Purpose
To define the exact criteria and ideas for freebies that successfully convert ADHD audiences into email subscribers.

## The "2-Minute Win" Rule
An ADHD lead magnet must provide a tangible sense of relief or accomplishment in under 2 minutes of use. If it requires setup, learning, or long-term commitment, it will fail.

## Winning Lead Magnet Ideas
1. **The "Brain Dump" Cheat Sheet**: A single-page PDF with prompting questions to get thoughts out of the head.
2. **The "Dopamine Menu" Template**: A pre-filled list of quick, medium, and slow dopamine-boosting activities.
3. **The "5-Minute Reset" Checklist**: A simple, step-by-step guide to un-overwhelm a messy desk or inbox.

## AI Execution Prompt
"Brainstorm 5 lead magnet ideas for the ADHD productivity niche. For each idea, evaluate it against the '2-Minute Win' rule. Select the best one and outline its exact contents and the title of the landing page."
