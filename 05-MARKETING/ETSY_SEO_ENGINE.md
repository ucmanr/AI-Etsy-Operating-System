# Etsy SEO Engine

## Purpose
To systematically optimize product listings for maximum visibility in Etsy search, balancing algorithmic relevance with human readability.

## Title Formula (Max 140 chars)
[Primary Keyword] + [Secondary Keyword] + [Target Audience] + [Use Case/Format] + [Unique Selling Proposition]
*Example*: "ADHD Daily Planner Digital | Neurodivergent Goodnotes Template | Minimalist Focus Tracker for Students | Instant Download PDF"

## Tag Strategy (Exactly 13 Tags)
- **Rule 1**: Use all 13 tags. No exceptions.
- **Rule 2**: Multi-word phrases are better than single words (e.g., "adhd planner" > "adhd").
- **Rule 3**: Front-load the most important keywords.
- **Rule 4**: Include 2-3 "ADHD-specific" or "neurodivergent" long-tail tags (e.g., "executive dysfunction", "dopamine menu").

## Keyword Placement Rules
1. **Title**: Primary keyword must be in the first 40 characters.
2. **Attributes**: Fill out ALL relevant Etsy attributes (Color, Occasion, Style) as they act as hidden search filters.
3. **Description**: Repeat the primary keyword naturally in the first 2 sentences.
