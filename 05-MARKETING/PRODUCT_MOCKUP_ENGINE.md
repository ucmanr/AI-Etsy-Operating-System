# Product Mockup Engine

## Purpose
To create a 10-image listing sequence that stops the scroll, clearly demonstrates value, and builds instant trust.

## The 10-Image Sequence Strategy
1. **Image 1 (The Hero)**: Clear, high-contrast shot of the product on a tablet/laptop. Large, readable text stating the core benefit. *Must pass the "thumbnail test" (readable at 10% size).*
2. **Image 2 (The Problem/Solution)**: Split screen. Left: "Overwhelmed by rigid planners?" Right: "Find your focus with flexible tracking."
3. **Image 3 (Feature Highlight 1)**: Close-up of the most unique feature (e.g., the "Dopamine Menu").
4. **Image 4 (Feature Highlight 2)**: Close-up of another key feature (e.g., "Brain Dump" section).
5. **Image 5 (How It Works)**: 3 simple steps: Download -> Import -> Use.
6. **Image 6 (Device Compatibility)**: Icons showing it works on iPad, Android, Desktop, and Printable.
7. **Image 7 (What's Included)**: Flat lay of all files/pages included in the download.
8. **Image 8 (Social Proof)**: Mockup of a glowing 5-star review (use placeholder text that highlights a key benefit).
9. **Image 9 (The Bundle/Upsell)**: "Pairs perfectly with our Weekly Review Tracker!"
10. **Image 10 (Brand Trust)**: "Instant Download | Lifetime Access | Friendly Support".

"Describe a Canva mockup for Image #[NUMBER] of an ADHD planner listing. Specify the background color (use off-white #FAFAFA), the device frame (e.g., iPad Pro), the placement of the product screenshot, and the exact, large, sans-serif text overlay to use."
