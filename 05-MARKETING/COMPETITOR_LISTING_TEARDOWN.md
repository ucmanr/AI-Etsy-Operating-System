# Competitor Listing Teardown

## Purpose
To reverse-engineer top-performing competitor listings to identify their winning SEO and conversion tactics, and find their weaknesses.

## Teardown Template
- **Competitor Name/URL**: 
- **Estimated Monthly Sales**: 
- **Title Analysis**: What keywords are they front-loading?
- **Tag Analysis**: (Use eRank or manual search to guess their top 5 tags).
- **Image Sequence**: What is their Image 1 hook? What is missing from their mockups?
- **Description Weaknesses**: Is it a wall of text? Do they fail to address a specific ADHD pain point?
- **Review Gaps**: What are customers complaining about in their 3-star reviews?

## Actionable Output
For every teardown, generate a "Our Advantage" statement. 
*Example*: "Competitor X has a great design but their description is a wall of text and they don't mention Goodnotes compatibility. Our Advantage: We will use scannable bullet points and explicitly target 'Goodnotes 6' in our title."
