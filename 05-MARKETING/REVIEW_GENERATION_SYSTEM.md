# Review Generation System

## Purpose
To ethically and systematically encourage happy customers to leave 5-star reviews, which is the #1 driver of Etsy search ranking and conversion.

## The "Frictionless" Review Strategy
1. **The Product Itself**: The #1 driver of good reviews is a product that works flawlessly and includes a "Thank You" page with clear, simple instructions.
2. **The Follow-Up File**: Include a beautifully designed PDF in the download named "Start Here & Get a Bonus". Inside, politely ask for a review and offer a small incentive (e.g., "Reply to this email with your review screenshot for a free sticker pack").
3. **Etsy Message Automation**: Set up an automatic Etsy message to trigger 7 days after purchase: 
   *"Hi [Name], I hope your new planner is helping you find your focus! If you have a moment, a 5-star review helps our small shop immensely. If you have any issues, reply here and I'll fix it immediately!"*

## Handling Negative Reviews
- **Rule**: Never argue. 
- **Response Template**: "I'm so sorry this didn't work for you. That's not the experience we want to provide. Please message me directly so I can issue a full refund or help you find a better solution." (This shows future buyers you are safe to purchase from).

## AI Prompt for Manus
"Draft a polite, warm, and concise Etsy follow-up message to be sent 7 days after purchase, asking for a review. Also, draft a professional, empathetic response template for a hypothetical 2-star review complaining about 'file formatting'."
