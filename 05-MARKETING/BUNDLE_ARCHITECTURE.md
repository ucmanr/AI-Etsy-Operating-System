# Bundle Architecture

## Purpose
To systematically combine existing products into higher-value offers, increasing Average Order Value (AOV) and making the shop more resilient.

## The Product Ladder Strategy
1. **The Starter Bundle**: 2 complementary core products. 
   *Example*: Daily Planner + Habit Tracker. (Priced at 1.5x the cost of a single item).
2. **The Ultimate System**: 4+ products, including templates, guides, and bonus assets (e.g., digital stickers). 
   *Example*: The Complete ADHD Focus System (Daily + Weekly + Monthly + Brain Dump + Sticker Pack). (Priced at 2.5x the cost of a single item).

## Rules for Bundling
- **Thematic Consistency**: All items in a bundle must share the exact same design system (colors, fonts, layout).
- **Clear Value Proposition**: The bundle must solve a *larger* problem than the individual parts. (e.g., "The Ultimate System" solves "total life organization", not just "daily planning").
- **Cross-Promotion**: Every single product listing must have an image (Image 9) promoting the relevant bundle.
