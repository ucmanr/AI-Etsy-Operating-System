# Competitor Traffic Mining

## Purpose
To reverse-engineer where successful competitors are getting their external traffic, allowing us to replicate and outperform their acquisition channels.

## The Mining Protocol
1. **Pinterest Audit**: Search the competitor's shop name or product titles on Pinterest. Which of their pins have the most saves/repins? What keywords are in those pin titles?
2. **TikTok/Instagram Audit**: Search their brand name on TikTok. Are they posting? Are there affiliate creators posting about them? What hashtags are they using?
3. **Backlink Check**: Use a free tool (like Ubersuggest) to see if any blogs or websites are linking to their Etsy shop or product.

## The Actionable Output
For every competitor analyzed, document:
- Their top-performing external traffic source.
- The specific keywords or hooks they are using.
- One actionable way we can create a *better* version of that traffic source.

## AI Execution Prompt
"Act as a Traffic Analyst. Outline a step-by-step plan to audit a competitor's Pinterest, TikTok, and backlink profile to discover their primary external traffic sources. Provide a template for logging the findings."
