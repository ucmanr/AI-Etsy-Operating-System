# Social Proof Distribution System

## Purpose
To systematically leverage customer reviews and user-generated content (UGC) to build instant trust and lower the barrier to purchase for new visitors.

## The "Review-to-Asset" Workflow
1. **Identify**: Find a 5-star review that mentions a specific, emotional benefit (e.g., "This planner finally made me feel like I have my life together").
2. **Design**: Open Canva. Place a beautiful, high-quality mockup of the product in the background (slightly blurred or darkened).
3. **Overlay**: Place the exact quote from the review in large, readable, high-contrast typography in the center. Add 5 gold stars.
4. **Distribute**: Post this graphic to Pinterest, Instagram Stories, and Twitter. 

## The "UGC" Incentive
Include a note in your follow-up email: "Love your planner? Post a photo of your setup on Instagram/TikTok, tag us, and we'll send you a 30% off coupon for your next purchase!"

## AI Execution Prompt
"Take these 3 customer reviews: [PASTE REVIEWS]. Select the most emotionally resonant quote. Then, write a Canva design brief for turning this quote into a high-converting social proof graphic, specifying background, typography, and layout."
