# Figma AI & Plugin Prompts

## Purpose
Standardized prompts for advanced users or AI plugins (like Musho AI or Wireframe Designer) in Figma to rapidly prototype digital products.

## Prompt 1: Wireframe Generation (Musho AI / Wireframe Designer)
"Generate a clean, minimalist wireframe for a digital ADHD daily planner. 
Sections required: 
1. Header with Date and 'Top 3 Priorities' (large). 
2. A flexible time-blocking area (2-hour increments, not 30-min). 
3. A 'Brain Dump' section with dotted lines. 
4. A 'Dopamine Menu' footer. 
Style: High contrast, sans-serif typography, generous whitespace, calming aesthetic. No decorative illustrations."

## Prompt 2: Component Creation
"Create a reusable 'Gentle Checkbox' component. It should be a 24x24px rounded rectangle with a 2px stroke in #CBD5E0. Create a 'Checked' variant where the fill is #68D391 and the stroke is #2F855A, with a simple white checkmark icon inside."

## Rule
Always apply the `04-DESIGN/COLOR_SYSTEM.md` and `TYPOGRAPHY_GUIDE.md` styles to the generated frames immediately after AI generation.
