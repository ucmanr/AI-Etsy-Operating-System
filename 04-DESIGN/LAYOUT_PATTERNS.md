# Layout Patterns Library

## Purpose
Standardized, pre-validated page structures that can be rapidly assembled to create new products.

## Pattern 1: The "Daily Focus" Page
- **Top (20%)**: Date, Top 3 Absolute Priorities (large checkboxes), Mood/Energy tracker.
- **Middle (60%)**: Time-blocking schedule (simplified, 2-hour blocks, not 30-min) OR a "Brain Dump" area.
- **Bottom (20%)**: "Dopamine Menu" (quick wins), Hydration, Tomorrow's 1 Priority.

## Pattern 2: The "Project Breakdown" Page
- **Top**: Project Name, Ultimate Goal, "Why this matters" (motivation anchor).
- **Middle**: 3-5 Major Milestones (not 20 tiny steps).
- **Bottom**: "Next Physical Action" (the very first, tiny step to take).

## Pattern 3: The "Habit Tracker" Page
- Grid layout with days of the month on the X-axis and 3-5 habits on the Y-axis.
- *Rule*: Never exceed 5 habits per page to prevent overwhelm.
