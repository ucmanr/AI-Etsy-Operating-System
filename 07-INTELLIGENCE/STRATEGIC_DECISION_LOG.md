# Strategic Decision Log

## Purpose
To record *why* major strategic directions were chosen, preventing future AI sessions from losing context or suggesting previously rejected ideas.

## Decision Log Template
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Decision**: [e.g., Enter the "ADHD Student Digital Planner" micro-niche]
- **Reasoning**: 
  - Strong pain intensity identified in review mining.
  - Search demand exists with manageable competition.
  - Competitor complaints show a clear gap in "flexible" layouts.
- **Rejected Alternatives**: 
  - Wedding planners (Too saturated, high customer service burden).
  - Generic habit trackers (Low perceived value, race to the bottom on price).
- **Confidence Level**: Medium
- **Next Review Date**: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 90 days)

## AI Execution Rule
Before proposing a new niche or major pivot, the AI must check this log. If an idea was previously rejected, the AI must explicitly state: "This was rejected on [Date] due to [Reason]. Has new evidence emerged to change this?"
