# Experiment Intelligence Framework

## Purpose
To ensure every strategic change is treated as a controlled, measurable experiment, not a random guess.

## The Experiment Template
- **Hypothesis**: (e.g., "Increasing price from $9.99 to $12.99 will increase perceived value and maintain conversion rate.")
- **Reasoning**: (e.g., "Premium competitors use stronger branding and charge more.")
- **Experiment Duration**: (e.g., "14 days")
- **Metrics to Measure**: (e.g., "Conversion rate, Revenue per visitor, Favorites, Refunds")
- **Decision Rule**: 
  - *Keep*: If Revenue per visitor increases by >10%.
  - *Modify*: If CVR drops slightly but total revenue is flat.
  - *Reject*: If CVR drops by >30%.

## AI Execution Prompt
"Design a 14-day experiment to test the hypothesis: '[HYPOTHESIS]'. Fill out the Experiment Template, including specific, measurable Decision Rules."
