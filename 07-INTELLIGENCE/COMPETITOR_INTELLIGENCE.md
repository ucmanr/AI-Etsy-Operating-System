# Competitor Intelligence Protocol

## Purpose
To reverse-engineer top-performing competitor listings to understand *why* they win, not just to copy them.

## The "Why" Analysis
Do not just extract keywords. Answer these deeper questions:
1. **Why does this listing rank?** (Is it age, review count, exact keyword match, or external traffic?)
2. **Why do customers buy?** (What specific emotional trigger or pain point does the copy hit?)
3. **What objections remain?** (What doubts do 3-star reviews reveal that the listing failed to address?)
4. **What opportunity exists?** (How can we combine the strengths of Competitor A with the missing features desired by Competitor B's customers?)

## AI Execution Prompt
"Analyze this competitor listing: [URL or Description]. Do not just list keywords. Answer the 4 'Why' questions in the Competitor Intelligence Protocol. Conclude with one specific 'Opportunity' we can exploit that they are missing."
