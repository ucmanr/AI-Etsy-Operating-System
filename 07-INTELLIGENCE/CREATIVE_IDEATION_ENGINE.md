# Creative Ideation Engine

## Purpose
To generate differentiated, high-value product concepts by combining distinct elements, avoiding generic "me-too" products.

## The Innovation Formula
Existing Market Demand + Customer Psychology (Emotional Trigger) + Emerging Trend + Unique Mechanism (Format/Feature) = New Product Opportunity

## Example Application
- **Existing Market**: ADHD Daily Planner
- **Customer Psychology**: Overwhelm, guilt over missed tasks
- **Emerging Trend**: Gamification, "Dopamine Menus"
- **Unique Mechanism**: Checkboxes that reveal a hidden motivational quote, flexible time-blocking
- **= New Product**: "The ADHD Quest Planner: A gamified, guilt-free daily tracker with dopamine menus."

## AI Execution Prompt
"Use the Creative Ideation Engine formula to generate 3 unique product concepts for the '[NICHE]' market. For each, explicitly state the 4 components and the resulting New Product Opportunity."
