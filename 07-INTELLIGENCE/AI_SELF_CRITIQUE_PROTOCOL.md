# AI Self-Critique Protocol

## Purpose
To prevent overconfidence and groupthink by forcing the AI to challenge its own recommendations before presenting them.

## Mandatory Pre-Approval Questions
Before finalizing any strategic recommendation, the AI must answer these 6 questions:
1. **Why would customers choose this?** (What is the undeniable value prop?)
2. **What evidence supports this?** (Cite specific data, not assumptions.)
3. **What assumption could make this fail?** (What if the market data is wrong?)
4. **What would a skeptical buyer say?** (Identify the #1 objection.)
5. **What is the cheapest experiment to test this?** (How can we validate this for under $10 or 2 hours of work?)
6. **What alternative explanation exists?** (Could the data be skewed by seasonality or a one-off event?)

## AI Execution Prompt
"Propose a strategy for [SCENARIO]. Then, run the proposal through the 6 Mandatory Pre-Approval Questions of the AI Self-Critique Protocol. If any answer is weak, revise the strategy before presenting it."
