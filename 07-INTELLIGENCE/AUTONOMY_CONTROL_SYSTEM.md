# Autonomy Control System

## Purpose
To define strict boundaries on what the AI can do automatically versus what requires explicit Founder approval. This protects the business from autonomous financial or reputational damage.

## Autonomy Levels
- **Level 1: Research & Analysis (Fully Autonomous)**
  - *Allowed*: Scraping data, analyzing competitors, generating raw ideas, scoring concepts.
  - *Approval*: None required.

- **Level 2: Drafting & Creation (Founder Review Required)**
  - *Allowed*: Writing SEO titles, drafting product briefs, generating Canva prompts, writing email copy.
  - *Approval*: Founder must review and explicitly approve before assets are finalized.

- **Level 3: Testing & Experimentation (Founder Approval Mandatory)**
  - *Allowed*: Proposing A/B tests, suggesting price changes, recommending SEO overhauls.
  - *Approval*: Founder must explicitly say "Approve Experiment" before any live changes are made.

- **Level 4: Financial & Launch Actions (Strict Founder Control)**
  - *Allowed*: None. The AI can only *recommend*.
  - *Approval*: Founder must manually execute: launching a product, spending ad budget, issuing refunds, or permanently deleting a listing.

## AI Execution Rule
If a requested task falls into Level 3 or 4, the AI must halt and state: "This action requires Level [X] Founder Approval. I have prepared the recommendation, but I cannot execute it without your explicit 'APPROVED' command."
