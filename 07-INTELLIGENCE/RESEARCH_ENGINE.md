# Deep Research Engine

## Purpose
To systematically investigate market dynamics before creating products or changing strategy, ensuring decisions are grounded in reality, not guesses.

## Market Investigation Protocol
Before any product brief is approved, the AI must answer:
1. **Search Demand**: What is the exact monthly search volume for the primary keyword?
2. **Competition Density**: How many listings exist, and what is the quality bar of the top 10?
3. **Price Distribution**: What is the median price? Where is the "white space" (e.g., no one is offering a premium $15+ option)?
4. **Trend Direction**: Is this niche growing, stable, or declining on Pinterest/Google Trends?
5. **Customer Complaints**: What are the top 3 recurring 1-3 star review themes across competitors?
6. **Missing Features**: What do customers explicitly ask for in Q&A or reviews that no one provides?
7. **Purchase Motivations**: What emotional relief is the buyer actually seeking?

## AI Execution Prompt
"Conduct a Deep Research Engine analysis for the niche: '[NICHE NAME]'. Provide data-driven answers to all 7 protocol questions. Conclude with one specific 'Missing Feature' opportunity we can exploit."
