# Review Analysis System

## Purpose
To turn customer feedback into a continuous product improvement loop, ensuring V2 of every product is objectively better than V1.

## The "Voice of Customer" Extraction
1. **Monthly Sweep**: Export all new reviews for the month.
2. **Categorize**: 
   - *Praise*: What specific words do they use to describe the value? (Add these to the Listing Copy).
   - *Confusion*: What did they struggle to understand? (Add this to the "Start Here" PDF).
   - *Feature Requests*: What did they wish it had? (This is the V2 Product Brief).

## The V2 Upgrade Rule
No product gets a "Version 2" update unless it has at least 3 data points (reviews, support messages, or low conversion metrics) indicating a specific, fixable flaw.

## AI Prompt for Manus
"Analyze these 15 customer reviews for [PRODUCT NAME]: [PASTE REVIEWS]. Extract: 1) Top 3 phrases of praise to add to our listing, 2) Any points of confusion to fix in the instructions, 3) One concrete feature request for Version 2.0."
