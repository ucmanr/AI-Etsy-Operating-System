# KPI Framework: What Actually Matters

## Purpose
To filter out vanity metrics and focus exclusively on the data points that directly correlate with revenue and sustainable growth.

## Primary KPIs (Check Weekly)
1. **Conversion Rate (CVR)**: (Orders / Views) * 100. 
   - *Target*: > 2.5%. 
   - *Action*: If < 1.5% after 100 views, the listing images or price are failing.
2. **Favorites-to-Views Ratio**: (Favorites / Views) * 100.
   - *Target*: > 8%.
   - *Action*: If high favorites but low sales, the price is too high or the checkout friction is too high.
3. **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Total Revenue / Total Orders.
   - *Target*: > $12.
   - *Action*: If AOV is low, push the "Starter Bundle" more aggressively in Image 9.

## Secondary KPIs (Check Monthly)
- **Return Customer Rate**: % of buyers who purchase a second time. (Target: > 15%)
- **Review Velocity**: Number of new 5-star reviews per week. (Target: > 2/week per active listing)
