# Competitor Analysis Framework

## Purpose
To deconstruct top-performing competitor products to identify their winning elements and exploitable weaknesses.

## Inputs
- URLs of top 3-5 competing Etsy listings.
- Access to their product images, descriptions, and reviews.

## Execution Steps
1. **Listing Teardown**: Document their Title structure, Tag usage, and Image sequence (e.g., Image 1: Hook, Image 2: Features, Image 3: Social Proof).
2. **Pricing Analysis**: Note base price, sale price, and bundle offerings.
3. **Review Mining**: Extract all 1, 2, and 3-star reviews. Categorize complaints (e.g., "too small", "confusing layout", "fonts hard to read").

## Output
- Competitor Teardown Matrix (Markdown Table).
- List of 3-5 "Unmet Needs" to target in our product.
