# Review Mining Framework

## Purpose
To systematically extract product improvement ideas directly from competitor customer feedback.

## Inputs
- 20-30 recent reviews (mix of 5-star and 1-3 star) from top competitors.

## Execution Steps
1. **5-Star Analysis**: What do customers *love*? (These are "Table Stakes" features we MUST include).
2. **1-3 Star Analysis**: What do customers *hate* or find confusing? (These are our "Differentiation Opportunities").
3. **Language Extraction**: Note the exact words customers use (e.g., "overwhelming", "cluttered", "lifesaver"). Use these exact words in our listing copy.

## Output
- Table Stakes Checklist.
- Differentiation Opportunity List.
- Customer Vocabulary Bank.
