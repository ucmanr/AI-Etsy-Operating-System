# Trend Discovery Engine

## Purpose
To identify rising trends in the ADHD/productivity space before they become oversaturated.

## Inputs
- Pinterest Trends data.
- TikTok/Instagram hashtag growth (e.g., #ADHDhacks, #Neurospicy).
- Google Trends 12-month data.

## Execution Steps
1. **Cross-Platform Validation**: A trend must be rising on at least 2 platforms (e.g., Pinterest + TikTok).
2. **Commercial Intent Check**: Search the trend on Etsy. Are there < 500 listings? If yes, it's an early opportunity.
3. **Longevity Check**: Is this a fleeting meme, or a sustained behavioral shift (e.g., "Dopamine menu" vs. a specific viral dance)?

## Decision Gate
- **Green Light**: Rising on 2+ platforms, < 500 Etsy listings, sustained behavioral relevance.
- **Red Light**: Only trending on one platform, or > 2,000 Etsy listings already.
