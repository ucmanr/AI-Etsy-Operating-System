# Niche Scoring Engine

## Purpose
A rapid, quantitative method to score a *broad niche* before diving into specific product ideas.

## Scoring Matrix (Max 50 Points)
- **Search Volume (15 pts)**: >5k/mo (15), 1k-5k/mo (10), <1k/mo (5)
- **Competition Level (15 pts)**: <1k listings (15), 1k-5k listings (10), >5k listings (5)
- **Profit Margin Potential (10 pts)**: Can be priced >$10 with low effort (10), $5-$10 (5), <$5 (0)
- **Trend Trajectory (10 pts)**: Rising rapidly (10), Stable (5), Declining (0)

## Decision Gate
- **40-50 Points**: Green Light. Proceed to Product Idea Generation.
- **25-39 Points**: Yellow Light. Requires a highly unique angle or micro-niche focus.
- **< 25 Points**: Red Light. Abandon niche.

## AI Prompt for Manus
"Score the '[NICHE NAME]' niche using the Niche Scoring Engine. Provide a point breakdown for Search Volume, Competition, Profit Margin, and Trend Trajectory. Conclude with a Green, Yellow, or Red Light recommendation and a 1-sentence justification."
