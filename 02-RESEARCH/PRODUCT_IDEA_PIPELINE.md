# Product Idea Pipeline

## Purpose
The standardized workflow for moving a raw product concept from brainstorming to a validated, CEO-approved brief.

## Pipeline Stages
1. **Stage 1: Raw Ideation** (Brainstorm 10-20 ideas based on Pain Point Library & Trend Engine).
2. **Stage 2: Quick Filter** (Discard ideas that violate `BUSINESS_RULES`, e.g., too complex, direct clones).
3. **Stage 3: Deep Validation** (Run top 3 ideas through `PRODUCT-VALIDATION-SCORECARD.md`).
4. **Stage 4: CEO Approval** (Present top 1-2 scoring ideas to CEO Agent for final go/no-go).
5. **Stage 5: Briefing** (Hand off approved idea to Product Manager Agent for `PRODUCT_BRIEF_TEMPLATE.md`).
