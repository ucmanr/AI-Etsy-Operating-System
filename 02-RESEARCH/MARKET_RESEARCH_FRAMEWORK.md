# Market Research Framework

## Purpose
To systematically evaluate the total addressable market (TAM) and viability of a digital product niche before any design work begins.

## Inputs
- Target niche keyword (e.g., "ADHD daily planner").
- Top 5 competitor listings on Etsy.
- eRank or Marmalead search volume data.

## Execution Steps
1. **Demand Check**: Verify monthly search volume is > 1,000 for primary keyword.
2. **Supply Check**: Verify total competing listings are < 5,000 (or < 1,000 for micro-niches).
3. **Revenue Check**: Identify at least 3 competitors with > 100 sales in the last 6 months.
4. **Gap Identification**: Note what is missing (e.g., "All top sellers are dark mode; none offer light mode with high contrast").

## Decision Gate
- **Pass**: Demand > 1k, Supply < 5k, 3+ profitable competitors, clear gap identified.
- **Fail**: Market is oversaturated (>10k listings) or demand is too low (<500 searches/mo).

## AI Prompt for Manus
"Analyze the Etsy market for '[NICHE KEYWORD]'. Provide: 1) Estimated monthly search volume, 2) Number of competing listings, 3) Top 3 competitor strengths, 4) Top 3 competitor weaknesses (based on review gaps). Format as a markdown table."
