# Customer Pain Point Library

## Purpose
A living document to catalog the specific, visceral frustrations of the ADHD target audience, ensuring our products solve real problems.

## Structure (Template for new entries)
- **Pain Point**: (e.g., "I buy planners but abandon them by week 2 because they are too rigid.")
- **Emotional Trigger**: (e.g., "Guilt, feeling broken, wasted money.")
- **Current Failed Solutions**: (e.g., "Complex bullet journals, highly structured corporate planners.")
- **Our Product's Answer**: (e.g., "A flexible, 'menu-based' daily planner with no guilt for skipping days.")

## AI Prompt for Manus
"Act as an ADHD customer. Describe your top 3 daily frustrations regarding [TOPIC, e.g., time management]. For each, describe the emotional toll and what a 'dream solution' would look like. Format using the Pain Point Library template."
