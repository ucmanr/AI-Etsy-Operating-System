# Keyword Research Framework

## Purpose
To identify high-intent, low-competition search terms to build the product and listing around *before* design begins.

## Inputs
- Seed keyword (e.g., "ADHD planner").
- Etsy search bar autocomplete suggestions.

## Execution Steps
1. **Seed Expansion**: Type seed keyword into Etsy search and record the top 5 autocomplete suggestions.
2. **Long-Tail Extraction**: Combine seed with modifiers: "for students", "digital", "Goodnotes", "minimalist", "printable".
3. **Scoring**: Assign each keyword a score: High Volume (10 pts) + Low Competition (10 pts) + High Relevance (5 pts).

## Output
- Primary Keyword (for Title front-load).
- 10-15 Secondary Long-Tail Keywords (for Tags and Description).
