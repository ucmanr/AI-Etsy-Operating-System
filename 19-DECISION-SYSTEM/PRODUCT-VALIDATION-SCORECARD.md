# Product Validation Scorecard (The 85/100 Gate)

Every product idea must be scored against these 5 pillars. **Total score must be >= 85 to proceed to design.**

## 1. Demand Score (Max 25 pts)
- [ ] High search volume on Etsy/Pinterest (10 pts)
- [ ] Clear, recurring pain point addressed (10 pts)
- [ ] Evergreen potential vs. fleeting trend (5 pts)
- *Subtotal: ___ / 25*

## 2. Competition Score (Max 25 pts)
- [ ] Low saturation of *high-quality* offerings (10 pts)
- [ ] Identified gap in competitor 1-star reviews we can fill (10 pts)
- [ ] Room for a unique angle or bundle (5 pts)
- *Subtotal: ___ / 25*

## 3. Profit & Difficulty Score (Max 25 pts)
- [ ] High perceived value allowing $10- price point (10 pts)
- [ ] Low production time (< 4 hours for initial asset) (10 pts)
- [ ] Zero ongoing maintenance or shipping costs (5 pts)
- *Subtotal: ___ / 25*

## 4. Brand Fit Score (Max 15 pts)
- [ ] Aligns perfectly with ADHD productivity/cognitive load reduction (10 pts)
- [ ] Aesthetically matches our minimalist, calming brand guidelines (5 pts)
- *Subtotal: ___ / 15*

## 5. Bundle Potential Score (Max 10 pts)
- [ ] Can easily be combined with 2+ other products for a premium offer (10 pts)
- *Subtotal: ___ / 10*

---
**TOTAL SCORE: ___ / 100**

### Decision Gate:
- **85 - 100**: ✅ APPROVED. Proceed to Product Briefing.
- **70 - 84**: ⚠️ HOLD. Requires refinement or better angle. Return to Ideation.
- **< 70**: ❌ REJECTED. Log in 18-KNOWLEDGE-BASE/FAILED-EXPERIMENTS and move on.
